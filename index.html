Demographic Info

These interactive charts will visualize the different types of bacteria collected from the volunteer's belly buttons. To use these tools use the drop down menu to select a volunteer's ID. Selecting this ID updates the bar chart and shows the data of the bacteria cultures found. The gauge chart shows how often the volunteer washes their belly button. The size of the bubbles in the bubble chart show the concentration and diverssity of bacteria that was in the volunteer's belly button.
